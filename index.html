iSWIM is a subsidiary of TRM (HK) Limited. We have more than 20 years experience in handling the employees’ compensation claims in Hong Kong. We understand the difficulties and headache in handling the claims. As a result, we collaborate with Hong Kong Polytechnic University to develop a smart work injury management system (SWIM). Original Document Handling
Handling and storage of original documents always a headache for insurers. We provide a solution to handle the original documents using the state-of-the-art technology- “Block Chain”.
The original documents will be scanned and kept at a safe place. An QR code will be stamped on the “true copy” and passed to the related parties.
Insurers can extract the data from the documents, retrieve a copy of the original copy, retrieve the original documents. All the handling of the documents will be logged and cannot be amended.
Workflow can be kicked started to ensure the proper handling based on the document type. The documents as well as the data can be embedded with the workflow for reference.
Data Integration
Insurers need to extract the data from the paper documents to process the claim. In the process, it involves a lot of manpower as well as having human error. iSWIM develop a technology to extract the data from the documents and stored in a blockchain database.
The data can pass to the insurers through data files or API. It will reduce human error during repeated data entries.
We can check the data from various sources such as Labour Department, Hospital Authority etc to ensure the data integrity. It helps to identify the fraudulent claims.
Data Visualization
Data is important in the claims handling. However, it will be hard to visualize the pattern if the data becomes big. iSWIM adopts various data visualization techniques to present it in an easily understand format.
Insurers can identify different patterns, especially for those exceptional claims pattern. It helps to identify those doctor shoppers, unattended cases or fraudulent claims.

Smart Work injury management
In 2018, TRM collaborated with PolyU to apply for innovative grant to develop a Smart Work Injury Management platform (“SWIM”). Based on more than 100,000 cases from various industries, the platform utilized the artificial intelligence technology (“AI”) to predict the sick leave duration and percentage of permanent disability.
At the end, it can develop a model for managing the work injury claim from the first day of injuries until the case closure. The most important of all, it helps facility proactive rehabilitation and early return to work. At the end, the insurers can save cost in employees’ compensation claim.

FEATURES
Storage

Original documents are scanned and kept in a safe place.

Copies

A QR code will be stamped on the “true copy” and passed to relevant parties.

Retrieval

Clients can retrieve copies or the original documents. All handling of documents is logged securely.

Browsing

Useful data can be extracted from the documents and passed to clients through an API or in formats like JSON or CSV.

WORKFLOW
Original documents are scanned and uploaded to our system with a reference and location code. Relevant data is extracted and updated in our system as well.
Clients will receive copies with QR codes pasted on the corner. Clients can scan the QR code to access information on the document.
Clients can request data on the document, ask for another copy, or request the originl document.
